LEGACY

Price:Rs275

Description:

It is said that a daughter may outgrow your lap, but she will never outgrow your heart. In Legacy, noted journalist and author Sudha Menon brings forth a rare collection of personal and evocative letters from parents to their daughters. Through their fearless approach to life, love, and overcoming obstacles, these icons from the world of business, arts, films, food, and sports share with us their experience and wisdom as they pass them on to their daughters.

Deeply moving and thought provoking, Legacy is a remarkable collection of life lessons that will delight and inspire at the same time.
